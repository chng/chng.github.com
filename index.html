I am born in Ganzhou, Jiangxi Province, China. And now I am a graduated student in Dalian University of Technology. I am a Linux C/C++ programmer. My research interests includes wireless access control and wireless network measurement, especially with physical layer information.

Main Research Project

Bingxian Lu, Zhicheng Zeng, Lei Wang, Brian Peck, and Daji Qiao. CLaWa: Crowdsourced Location Aware Wi-Fi Access Control.

We have build a real-time platform to collect the channel state information. With this abundant information from the physical layer, we design location distinction models to enhance the wireless access control security.
